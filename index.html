Our Donation Plan

Your donation will support Plan International's vital work to provide food,shelter and child education.

We plan to provide the basic needs of life

Food for a family -

By donating food, we're feeding people, not landfills, supporting local communities, and saving all the resources that went into producing that food from going to waste.

Shelter for a family -

Shelter is a basic human need crucial for survival in cases of natural hazards or conflict. It provides security, personal safety and protection from the weather, and prevents ill health and disease.

Education for a child -

Education is a powerful tool for breaking the cycle of poverty; supporting child survival, growth, development and well-being; and closing the gap in social inequality.
